Connie Achtenberg

Email: connie.achtenberg@gmail.com
Phone: 734-883-9121
Rate: $40.00 per hour
Lesson Types: Field Moves , Freestyle , Ice Dance
Qualifications: Senior Moves in the Field, Novice Freestyle, Gold Pattern Dance, Senior Solo Free Dance. PSA Basic Accreditation

Background:

Connie has been an active AAFSC member since 1998. She performed in many Melody On Ice shows and USFS competitions, is a six-time National Solo Ice Dance Series Final qualifier in Senior Combined and Gold Pattern dance and is a multiple medalist at the US Adult National Championships. She also takes ballet at Ballet Chelsea and has been involved in their performances. 


She graduated from the University of Michigan with a degree in Music and played piano. While at U of M, she continued to be active in skating and was the Vice President of Freestyle for the University of Michigan Figure Skating Club, helped host Intercollegiate Nationals and produced the ice shows. She has competed in three Intercollegiate Team National Championships. 


She enjoys working with skaters of all levels and ages including beginners and helping them progress through the Basic Skills curriculum and taking them beyond into the USFS testing structure. 



Experience:

Connie has been teaching group lessons since 2012 at Yost Ice Arena (U of M), Chelsea Arctic Coliseum and at the Cube (AAFSC). She has worked with other coaches as a way to expand her knowledge before becoming a coach and teaching private lessons. She has taken skaters through the USFS testing curriculum and is eager to help them reach their goals. She is a USFS coach and a Professional Skaters Association (PSA) member with Basic Accreditation rating and Master Sport Science exam. She remains active in the skating community with volunteering, has a USFS Regional Official appointment and has served as a judge at many Basic Skills competitions. 




Is Coach Currently Available for Students?: Yes
