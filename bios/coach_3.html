Bradley Newman

Email: bradnewman50@gmail.com
Phone: 517-375-1982
Rate: $60.00 per hour
Lesson Types: Field Moves , Freestyle
Qualifications: Gold Freestyle, Gold Moves in the Field, Pre-Gold Dance, Juvenile Pair

Background:

Brad began skating at the age of 7 with the Lansing Skating Club in Lansing, Michigan where he trained for 11 years as a regional competitive skater. Brad has achieved his USFS Gold Freestyle, Gold Moves in the Field, Pre-Gold Dance, and Juvenile Pair.

Brad traveled as a professional figure skater with "Disney on Ice" for 5 years on three different productions (The Wizard of Oz, The Little Mermaid, and 100 Years of Disney Magic) on various national and international tours (North America, Central America, Asia, Australia, and Europe).

Brad completed his Bachelor of Arts in Interpersonal Communication at Western Michigan University and his Master of Arts in Higher Education/Student Personnel Administration from New York University. He works full-time at the University of Michigan where he serves as a Wellness and Grants Coordinator for MHealthy/Project Healthy Schools.


Experience:

Brad joined the AAFSC Learn to Skate coaching staff in May 2017 and the AAFSC Professional staff in January 2019. He currently serves as the Assistant Director of Competition Club and looks forward to helping skaters achieve their individual skating goals. 


Is Coach Currently Available for Students?: No
