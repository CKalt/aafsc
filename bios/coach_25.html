Melissa Keil

Email: melissakeil88@gmail.com
Phone: (734) 323-3600
Rate: $60.00 per hour
Lesson Types: Field Moves , Freestyle
Qualifications: Gold Field Moves, Gold Freestyle, Pre-Silver Dance PSA Basic Accreditation

Background:

Melissa began skating at the age of 2 and started competing freestyle at the age of 5. Her competitive career lasted for another 12 years. Her training took place in Michigan and at the World Arena in Colorado Springs, CO. During this time, she participated in five Regional competitions, was a 1999 Juvenile Freestyle Junior Nationals alternate and the 2004 Michigan High School Novice Freestyle State Champion. She also competed for the University of Michigan Figure Skating team as an undergraduate. In addition to her competitive accomplishments, she has her USFSA Gold Medals in Freestyle and Moves In The Field.


Outside the rink, Melissa is a dentist in Battle Creek, MI where she serves as Dental Director at a federally qualified public health center. Melissa earned both her bachelor's degree (2010) and Doctor of Dental Surgery degree (2018) from the University of Michigan.


Experience:


Melissa has been coaching group lessons since 2003 and private lessons since 2005. Her students have passed tests at the Senior level and successfully compete at competitions in and out of state. She was the head coach of Dexter/Manchester/Saline High School figure skating team from 2006 - 2008, leading them to the State Championships both seasons and the head coach of the Huron High School skating team from 2011-2014, leading them to the State Championships as well. She is a member in good standing of the PSA and the USFSA and continues to educate herself by taking PSA courses. Her passion is teaching freestyle and she enjoys helping skaters succeed at all levels. Along with promoting excellence and good sportsmanship on the ice, she also strives to teach her students lessons that will help them succeed in life outside the rink.




Is Coach Currently Available for Students?: Yes
