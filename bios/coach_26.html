Meredith Longoria

Email: icesk8rmere@aol.com
Phone: (517) 914-3824
Rate: $60.00 per hour
Lesson Types: Field Moves , Freestyle
Qualifications: Gold MIF, Jr Freestyle, Intermediate Pairs, Pre-Silver Dance, Certified Rated MIF, Certified Rated FS, Bachelors degree in Business Admin

Background:

Meredith competed up to the junior level in freestyle while also testing both dance and pairs. Her long time coach was Douglas Haw who also trained national and international competitors in the U.S. and Canada.
In 2006 she graduated from Siena Heights University with her Bachelor's degree in Business Administration.

Experience:

Meredith has over 15 years coaching experience and proudly joined Ann Arbor FSC in 2009 full time. She has coached skaters to their gold medal in moves in the field and has also trained regional competitors in freestyle. During her time with the club she has enjoyed directing Melody on Ice and also doing choreography for several shows. She has continued her coaches education by attending multiple conferences and achieving ratings in both Freestyle and Moves in the Field. She enjoys coaching skaters of all ages and abilities basic skills to senior level.
