Katharine Greene

Email: kathg@umich.edu
Phone: 3102706715
Rate: $60.00 per hour
Lesson Types: Field Moves , Freestyle , Off-Ice Conditioning
Qualifications: Senior Freestyle, Senior Moves in the Field

Background:

Education: BA in Philosophy, Psychology from the University of Michigan, Ann Arbor

Experience:

My objective as a coach is to share my passion for learning by teaching skaters to enjoy the process of acquiring new skills. I graduated from the University of Michigan, Ann Arbor with majors in philosophy and psychology and I am interested in the philosophy and psychology of learning. From my experience, the learning process is most effective when the student enjoys what he or she is doing and this enjoyment motivates the student to continue and take on further challenges. I am interested in working with students of all ages and levels.
