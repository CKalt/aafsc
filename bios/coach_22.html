Marisa Hutchinson

Email: hutchinson.marisa@gmail.com
Phone:
Rate:
Lesson Types: Field Moves , Synchronized Skating
Qualifications: Senior Moves in the Field Gold Dance Intermediate FS 3-time National Champion

Background:

Marisa has been a member of the Ann Arbor Figure Skating club for 15 years. She was a member of Hockettes Synchronized Skating Team for 10 years. During these years, Marisa was part of the National Championship and Cup of Berlin Championship 2005 Junior team, captain of the 2007 National Championship team, and made two appearances at the Junior World Cup in 2005 and 2006.

 

After graduating high school, Marisa went on to Miami University where she competed with the Miami University Senior Synchronized Skating team for 4 years. She was acting captain her senior year and appeared at three World Synchronized Skating Championships. In 2009, her team won the Senior National Championships.


ONLY AVAILABLE FOR LESSONS IN THE SUMMER


Experience:

Marisa has been coaching the Hockettes since 2013, focused mainly with the Novice and Junior teams. She also coaches individual lessons in field moves and synchro skills during the summer months. Outside of coaching, Marisa works as a School Psychologist and Behavior Consultant in Livingston County Schools. 


Is Coach Currently Available for Students?: No
