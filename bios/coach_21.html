Lindsay Grajek

Email: lindsaygrajek@gmail.com
Phone: (517) 749- 5006
Rate: $60.00 per hour
Lesson Types: Field Moves , Synchronized Skating
Qualifications: 7 time National Champion, 4 Time World Medalist in Synchronized Skating

Background:

Lindsay began her skating career at a small club in Howell, MI ( Grand Oaks Ice Arena ) at the age of five. At the age of twelve she joined the Hockettes' Intermediate team. Lindsay skated for the Hockettes for five years, spending three of those years on the Junior team. She went on to skate for the Haydenettes for six seasons, earning six consecutive National titles, four World metals and the World Small Gold medal for the Long Program. During her stint on the Haydenettes the team also travelled around the world representing the United States at The Figure Skating Grand Prix, The Shanghai Trophy and The French cup and many other international competitions including six Synchronized Skating World Championships. 


Following her time on the Haydenettes Lindsay returned to Michigan to attend school at the University of Michigan. While at U Of M Lindsay was a member of their National Championship Collegiate synchro team. In 2019 she graduated with a B.S. in Biology, Health and Society. 

Experience:

Lindsay previously taught private lessons at Grand Oaks Ice arena in Howell, MI. After moving to Massachusetts she worked with multiple Hayden synchro teams, including Pre-Juvinille (Shooting Stars), Intermediate (Star Mates), Novice (Ice Mates) , Junior (Lexettes) and Collegiate (Metroettes). After returning to Michigan, Lindsay choreographed for Adrain College's Senior and Collegiate teams. 

Other coaching experience in the synchronized skating disciple include: two years at the Great Britain National Training camp in Scotland, multiple visits to The Northernettes synchro teams in Minnesota and guest coaching for the U of M Synchro Teams.
Lindsay is currently a Co-Coach for the Hockettes Intermediate team and the Head Coach of the Junior Team.

Lindsay served on U.S. Figure Skating's DREAM team for 3 seasons where she taught skating skills to students all around the country.

She loves helping her students to see their full potential, all while enjoying themselves on the ice.
