Lesson Types: Field Moves , Ice Dance , Synchronized Skating

Background:

Alison has been skating since the age of 5. She has skated and competed in freestyle, figures, field moves, dance, and synchronized team skating. She has skated on international senior synchronized teams, and she is presently am one of the coaches of our international junior team.

Alison is a student at Eastern Michigan University, majoring in Education.

Experience:

Alison has been teaching dance, moves in the field, and basic skills for over 20 years. She has been coaching synchro teams - all levels - since 1995.
