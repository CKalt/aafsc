Elizabeth Casey

Email: elizabethncasey@icloud.com
Phone: (847)971-5266
Rate: $22.00 per hour
Lesson Types: Field Moves , Freestyle
Qualifications:

Background:

Elizabeth began skating at the age of 4. Since then she has skated as a member of the Ann Arbor United High School Skating Team and as a singles freestyle skater. She has taken part in numerous regional freestyle competitions and finished second in the region at the open juvenile level. Elizabeth has competed in singles freestyle skating up to the senior level. Elizabeth hopes to instill the life lessons that skating taught her into her students. Elizabeth is planning on attending the University of Michigan and majoring in political science. 


Experience:

Elizabeth began volunteering as a part of the Learn To Skate organization at the age of 14 and became hired staff at 16. She is currently a AAFSC Junior coach and looking forward to taking on new students!
