Joseph Breeden

Email: josephmbreeden@yahoo.com
Phone: 410-428-2179
Rate: $48.00 per hour
Lesson Types: Field Moves , Freestyle
Qualifications: Senior Field Moves, Junior Freestyle, PSA FCC

Background:

Joseph began skating when he was nine years old, and has competed in freestyle, theater-on-ice, showcase, pairs, and synchro in USFS and ISI competitions. He currently specializes in field moves and singles freestyle. Joseph has competed at States Games, USFS Collegiate Championships, and USFS Adult Championships, and has participated in several ice shows, including playing the Scarecrow in "The Wizard of Oz on Ice" at his previous skating club. Joseph achieved his USFS Gold Medal in field moves during his junior year of college.

Joseph graduated with degrees in Aerospace Engineering and Mathematics in 2019 from the University of Maryland, where he was the Co-President of the University of Maryland Figure Skating Club for two years. He started coaching at the Gardens Figure Skating Club during his senior year. Joseph has worked as a Controls Systems Engineer at NASA, and is currently pursuing his PhD at the University of Michigan, where he studies Aerospace Controls Engineering. Joseph currently competes with the University of Michigan Figure Skating Club, and is an active coach and skater at the Ann Arbor Figure Skating Club.

Experience:

Joseph has coached Learn to Skate since 2019 at AAFSC and Gardens FSC. Joseph joined the AAFSC Junior Coaching Staff in 2021 and Pro-Staff in 2022. He has held a PSA Basic Accreditation since June 2019, and actively works to expand his coaching knowledgebase. To Joseph, skating is a unique combination of technique, artistry, athleticism, and resilience, and Joseph looks forward to helping skaters of all ages and abilities develop these qualities and have fun learning new skills. Joseph currently teaches Learn to Skate and private lessons for youth and adult skaters.



Is Coach Currently Available for Students?: Yes
