Rebecka Hakansson

Email: beckabubbles05@gmail.com
Phone: (734) 780-1672
Rate: $30.00 per hour
Lesson Types: Freestyle , Off-Ice Conditioning
Qualifications: JUNIOR COACH: Triple U.S. Gold Medalist: Moves In the Field, Gold Freestyle, Gold Free Dance and Pre-Gold Dance

Background:

Rebecka is originally from Ann Arbor Michigan and began skating with AAFSC at 6 years old. Since then, she has competed freestyle and dance representing AAFSC, including qualifying for Solo Dance Nationals and winning a freeskate pewter in the National Qualifying Series in 2021. She is completing her Senior year of High School, including being a captain for the Ann Arbor United High School team, and plans to become a coach full time.

Experience:

Rebecka started her coaching journey in 2019 at age 14 as an apprentice coach for AAFSC. She became a Junior coach for the club in 2022.
