Erin Donovan

Email: coacherindonovan@gmail.com
Phone: 734-476-7481
Rate: $60.00 per hour
Lesson Types: Field Moves , Ice Dance , Off-Ice Conditioning , Synchronized Skating
Qualifications: Senior MIF, Pre-Gold Dance, Int FS, World Team Member, U.S. National Champion Synchro Skater and Coach

Background:

I began skating at the age of 6 and immediately loved the sport. I was a competitive synchronized skater at the National level from 1991-2001. I also tested and competed in freestyle, dance, figures, and field moves. I then went to Miami University and was a member of the Varsity Synchronized Skating Team all four years of college as well as team captain and club president during my senior year. I graduated in 2001 with a degree in Engineering Management.

As a part of the Miami Team, I was a 3 time World Team Member, U.S. National Champion, and International Medalist, as well as Miami University Female Scholar Athlete of the Year in 2001.

I received my Novice Synchronized Skating Judge's appointment in 2001 and judged Pacific Coast Sectionals in 2002.


Experience:

I have coached for the past 24 years, the last 19 years here in Ann Arbor. I specialize in synchronized skating, field moves, ice dancing, and power skating. As a coach for the Ann Arbor Figure Skating Club, I work with skaters on field moves, stroking, and ice dancing. I have coached skaters past every level of field moves from pre-preliminary through senior moves. My student was the first to pass the new novice field moves in September of 2010. 

In 2017, I worked in Beijing, China to coach in the iSkating Synchronized Skating Team's exchange training program. In 2016 and 2017, I coached the Mexican National Senior Synchronized Skating team through 2 back to back World Synchronized Skating Championship appearances. While working with the team, I also coached individual skaters in Monterrey, Mexico on both field moves and choreography. I have also provided choreography for synchro teams around the country including most recently, Adrian College Senior Team and Rose City Crystals Intermediate (Pasadena, CA).

Prior to coming to Ann Arbor, I taught at the Miami University Summer Figure Skating School and Summer Synchronized Skating School for 5 years as well as at various camps and summer skating schools in the Baltimore - Washington area. In the summer of 1999, I traveled to Madrid, Spain to introduce Moves in the Field and Synchronized Skating to the skaters and coaches. I conducted Moves in the Field and power skating classes, taught ways to use field moves in choreography, gave private lessons in ice dancing, team taught freestyle to skaters of all levels along with the Spanish coaches, and started the first Spanish Synchronized Skating Team. 

As the director of the synchro program in Ann Arbor, I have taken the Hockettes Junior Synchronized Skating Team to two U.S. National Championship Titles and two trips to the World Challenge Cup for Juniors. I have coached teams to the U.S. National Championships in every level of the sport and earned numerous Midwestern Sectional Championship titles for the Ann Arbor Figure Skating Club. I have coached the University of Michigan Collegiate Synchro Team to 11 consecutive National medals including 3 National Championships. In 2005 I was nominated for the P.S.A/U.S.F.S Developmental Coach of the Year and Choreographer of the Year. I was named the 2005 Synchronized Team Coach of the Year by the same organizations. I have worked with the P.S.A. and U.S.F.S. in seminars across the country to promote improved edge quality and unique step sequences among our U.S. synchronized teams. Finally, I have attended educational seminars each year to expand my knowledge and improve my coaching technique in the areas of field moves, ice dance, stroking, and synchronized skating.
