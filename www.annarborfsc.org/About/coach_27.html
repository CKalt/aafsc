Nicole Falardeau

Email: Nicoleifss@aol.com
Phone: (734) 673-1418
Rate: $60.00 per hour
Lesson Types: Synchronized Skating
Qualifications: Senior MIF, Gold Freestyle, Gold Dance, Gold Figure, Canadian Gold Freestyle, Preliminary Pair

Background:

Nicole Falardeau has been a member of the Ann Arbor Figure Skating Club for fifteen years. She was a member of the Hockette junior team for one year and has been both an individual and a synchronized coach for the past fourteen years. Previous to this Nicole represented the Midland Figure Skating Club individually at Regionals for seven years and Midwesterns once. She also competed on their precision teams at both Midwesterns and Nationals. Nicole competed internationally with the senior Crystallettes for eight years which included one trip to Worlds.


Nicole has also taken various ballet, modern, tap, hip hop, ply metrics, Pilates, aerobic, yoga, and strength training classes for eighteen years which contributes to her knowledge of movement and choreography.


Nicole attended the University of Michigan and received her bachelor degree in Biology and her Master Degree in Education. She is certified to teach both biology and math and has been teaching high school math for the past ten years.


Experience:

Nicole coached various levels individually for six and a half years but is now focusing on synchronized skating. She has coached the Hockettes synchronized teams for the past fourteen years at several levels including Preliminary, Juvenile, Open Juvenile, Intermediate, Novice, and Junior. She also has been a member of the PSA for fourteen years.
Is Coach Currently Available for Students?: Yes
