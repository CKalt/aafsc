Claire Mifsud

Email: CLAIREMIFSUD16@GMAIL.COM
Phone: 832-859-0667
Rate: $60.00 per hour
Lesson Types: Field Moves , Freestyle , Off-Ice Conditioning
Qualifications: Gold Move in the Field, Pre-Gold Dance, Licensed Athletic Trainer

Background:

Claire began competing at the age of 8. Throughout her 12-year career, she took part in many different disciplines of skating including Singles, Ice Dance, Synchro, and Theater on Ice. She has over 8 years competing at her regions' Regional and Sectional competitions and represented Texas at the State Games of America. Claire is a Gold Medalist in Move in the Fields and a Pre-Gold Medalist in Ice Dance. She was a 2-time gold medalist with HITS competing at the Annual International Theater on Ice competition. Participating as both a skater and coach, she was an active member of her annual holiday show for over 10 years. In 2016, she graduated from Texas State University with her Bachelor of Science in Athletic Training and has been licensed and practicing for over 6 years. 

Experience:

Claire first began coaching as an assistant coach with her Learn to Skate program in Sugar Land, Texas. Within 6 months she was promoted to head coach and began taking on private lessons. She continued coaching in Texas for the next 6 years, before moving up to Michigan. She has coached numerous students from basic skills through high leveled freestyle, both on and off the ice. She currently works with UM Dearborn's Men's Hockey program, working on both on ice and off ice strength and conditioning. 
Is Coach Currently Available for Students?: Yes
