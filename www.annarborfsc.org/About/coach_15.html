Karolyn Flaherty

Email: celeste5902@icloud.com
Phone: 313-283-7348
Rate: $60.00 per hour
Lesson Types: Field Moves , Freestyle
Qualifications: US figure skating gold medalist in Dance. Coached regional champions in Intermediate and Juvenile. Experience as a skating director, staff organizer, and a ice show development head coach.

Background:

Karolyn has over 30 year of experience in coaching skaters of all ages and levels from basic skills through their Senior freestyle tests has had multiple medalists at the regional level. Karolyn has been a skating director for 4 rinks in Southeastern Michigan and also has experience as a staff organizer and a ice show development head coach. Her favorite thing about coaching is sharing her love of the sport with others, inspiring them to be better, watching them achieve their goals and seeing them carry their skills into life.
