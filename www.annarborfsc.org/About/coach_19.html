Kyra Christensen

Email: kyrabchristensen@gmail.com
Phone: (720)-653-0217
Rate: $30.00 per hour
Lesson Types: Field Moves , Freestyle
Qualifications: JUNIOR COACH , USFS Gold Medalist in Moves in the Field

Background:

Kyra began skating at the age of 8 in Medford, Oregon. She continued to train and compete as her family moved around the country, eventually landing in Michigan. She has skated on several of the Hockettes teams and plans to skate for the Umich Synchronized Skating Team this year. She is going into her sophomore year at the University of Michigan. Kyra enjoys working with students and hopes to help them achieve as many of their goals as possible.

Experience:

Kyra started coaching at 14 with the Columbine FSC in Colorado as a Junior Coach.  When she moved to Ann Arbor in 2020 she continued coaching with the AAFSC.
