Spring Learn to Skate


Registration for Spring Learn to Skate Classes is NOW AVAILABLE!

*Important UPDATES - Please READ*

You must be an Ann Arbor Figure Skating Club or Learn to Skate Member to participate in our classes. Membership is $35.00 annually and runs July 1, 2023 to June 30, 2024. 


Due to administrative limitations and safety concerns, we cannot allow make-up classes or prorating of classes. There will be no credits or refunds for missed classes.


Please make sure you register for the correct class. There will be a $10 change fee for each skater/class changed. Please double check your selection BEFORE submitting your registration.


Registration will be processed through EntryEeze with EACH level and day listed separately. Only classes for which your skater qualifies will be listed for registration.


Helmets will no longer be provided by the Ann Arbor Ice Cube. Skaters ages 3-6, those in Basic 1, and all who wish to wear a helmet must bring their own helmet to wear during class.


We are really looking forward to our Spring Learn to Skate Classes! Please contact our Learn to Skate Director, Claire Mifsud at clairemifsud16@gmail.com with any questions.

Information regarding Spring Ice Discounts

The Soring 2024 discount program will include ALL ice sessions Club ice AND Pass Thru ice.


The program will work as follows:


Each discount is based on a "single" EntryEeze shopping cart purchase that meets the following net totals.


5% - $500 to $749

7.5% - $750 to $999  

10% - $1,000+  


The discount will appear as a credit to your skaters' Entry Eeze account within 10 days of purchase and can be used to purchase future ice.


FAQ's

Q. May I combine multiple family members?

A. Yes, as long as it is one shopping cart purchase.

 

Q. May I combine two or more separate ice purchases to increase my discount level?

A. No, only single shopping cart totals will be used to determine discounts.

 

Q. Will I be able to include a mixture of morning ice along with late afternoon ice?

A. Yes, that is a mixture of Pass Thru ice and Club ice and that is OK.

 

Q. May I include my skater's LTS or Hockettes ice fees toward my totals?

A. No, this is for Club ice sessions and Pass Thru ice sessions only.

 

Q. I have multiple skaters in my family. Who will get the credit?

A. The skater with highest net cost in the shopping cart in question.

 

Q. We have credits in our skater's account. Will they apply toward total?

A. No, credits reflect previously paid ice, this discount is for new ice purchases only.

 

Q. How soon will the credit be applied to my skater's account?

A. As soon as is possible, but definitely within 10 business days.
